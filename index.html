    Information

    Everything you track stays on your phone. There is no server and no account — nothing is ever uploaded, and the app works fully offline. Anyone opening the app’s link just gets their own empty copy. To erase your data, use “Reset everything”.

    If a friend wants DoseTracker too, they can scan this code or type in the address:

    dosetracker.github.io

    Questions or feedback? Contact us at dosetracker@proton.me.

    DoseTracker is a logbook, not medical advice.
